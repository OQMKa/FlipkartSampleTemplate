The Flipkart Orders Analytics Dashboard transforms raw transactional data into visually rich insights.
It helps answer key business questions such as:
Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
What months perform best in terms of sales and profitability?
Which cities contribute the most to order volume?
How do customers prefer to pay?
Which sub-categories are the most and least profitable?
The goal of this dashboard is to simplify complex data into easily digestible visuals that support strategic planning.
